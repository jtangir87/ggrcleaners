Now Hiring!
Global Garment Restoration & Cleaners LLC. is looking to add Cleaning Technicians to our team. Join an industry leader!
General Summary

To clean, dry and restore property, clothing and contents damaged by fire, smoke, water, sewage, mold or other insurance losses to their original condition by performing the following duties. Other duties may be assigned.

  • Must keep facility and all work areas clean daily
  • Know how to use cleaning chemicals and equipment in a safe, effective manner.
  • Drive van/truck, stock & clean van, pick up materials for jobs.
  • Packing/unpacking property, moving boxes from shop to warehouse, inventory of items, keeping a log;
  • Carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning, clean draperies, fan and dehumidifier set up, use of negative air machine.
  • Move and/or cover furniture clean building structure.
  • Must possess the ability to complete paperwork (monitoring of drying equipment, time card tracking of tasks and jobs, read and follow work orders, track purchases on jobs).
  • Must be able to wear half and full-face respirator.
Experience/Skills Sought, but will train:
  • Ideal candidates will have experience in restoration work
  • The ideal candidate will have IICRC certifications
  • Ability to read the Xactimate scope of work
  • Use technology
  • Ability to lift 50 lbs.

Salary and Hours: $10-$12 hourly / 20-40+ hours weekly
